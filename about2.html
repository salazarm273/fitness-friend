    About

    If you want a guiding hand in staying mindful about fitness and living the life you want, you've come to the right place! Fitness Friend is here to be your friend, and give you helpful tools on your journey.

    Fitness Friend can keep track of how active you are and see how many calories you burn with the activity log, or monitor your calorie intake with the food log. Challenges and news will make excersizing and eating right fun.

    We'll also help guide you to a target weight if that's your thing, and you'll be able to track all the weight loss/gain you're hard work leads to.
